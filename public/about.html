About

  1. Homepage
  2. About

Growing up in the Netherlands, DJ Brusuro was exposed to a lot of different music styles and has played the guitar instrument himself. But It was Dancemusic that eventually won his hart and since then it took over his life. As this happened it became a duty to share his passion for music as a DJ and winning the hearts of a growing crowd!

Good music will always catch the surface and it is his purpose to give the crowd uplifting music but above all an experience that they didn't expect beforehand! Brusuro's style can be described as energetic and uplifting, where dance music soulfully meets the dancefloor and touches you.

With years behind the decks playing as a DJ comes great experience, his signature style of mixing and bringing fresh beats and mashups that will take the audience to a journey that they will want to keep going until the early morning!

Having played at numerous events in the Netherlands the last couple of years Brusuro took a challenge to win the dancefloors in other parts of Europe as well. He is playing regularly as a resident at Portus Beach Club Croatia and other venues in Croatia as well.

He is ready to take this musical trip to other continents as well. Who is ready to join this journey with him?
